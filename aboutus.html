About UNI Freelancers

From the heart of Kibera, we empower young people with digital skills and meaningful work — restoring dignity, unlocking potential, and transforming entire communities.

Our Story

Growing up in Kibera, we saw brilliant young people full of potential but trapped by unemployment and lack of opportunity. We founded UNI Freelancers to bridge that gap — helping youth build digital skills and earn dignified incomes online.
